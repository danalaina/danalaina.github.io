My name is Dan (Alaina) Wang, a Postdoc at the [University of Copenhagen](https://www.ku.dk/english/) associated with the [AI Pioneer Centre](https://www.aicentre.dk/), where I am advised by Professor [Serge Belongie](https://www.belongielab.org/). My current research interests lie in the intersection of Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Computer Graphics, with the objective of developing advanced AI techniques to improve human life. I received my Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the [University of British Columbia](https://www.ubc.ca/), where I was supervised by Professor [Z. Jane Wang](https://people.ece.ubc.ca/~zjanew/) and Professor [Tim Salcudean](https://bmiai.ubc.ca/people/tim-salcudean). My Ph.D. research focused on 3D computer vision and explainable AI. 

**Research interests**: 3D reconstruction, novel view synthesis, generative models, and explainable AI.
